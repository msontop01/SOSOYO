YOU ARE HIGHLY WELCOMED TO THE SCHOOL OF SCIENCE OYO OYO STATE NIGERIA

The school of science oyo (soso) is a top ranking school of science in Nigeria and indeed the nation's pride. Established in 1997, the school has grown tremendously, stretching its academic disciplines and research across six different schools and thirty academic departments. The School of Science is located in Oyo State, Nigeria
